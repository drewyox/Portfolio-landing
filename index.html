About Me

I grew up in California, but went to school at the University of Oregon South of Portland in Eugene.

After school I helped my father and a mutual friend start a business called Borrowshare. The idea of the company was airbnb for your stuff. You could rent out your belongings to your neighbors, strengthening community bonds, reducing waste through re-use of items, and serving as a small income source for those who needed it. In the early days of our project, we had no one to handle coding and tech for our rudimentary website. I taught myself the basics of html and css through codecademy in order to help out our company. Later, when we started working with a man named Dominic Merenda, he suggested I pursue coding more seriously. At the time I didn't think much of it, but two and a half years later I decided to follow up on that and attend Epicodus!
