About Me

I grew up in California, and moved all around Northern California. Most recently though, I lived in Santa Cruz California after I graduated from the University of Oregon. I lived there for about 3 years after graduating and moved to Portland in early 2018.

After school I helped my father and a mutual friend start a business. In the early days of our project, we had no one to handle coding and tech for our rudimentary and outsourced website. I taught myself the basics of html and css in order to help out. This was my first experience with coding, and I really liked it! Unfortunately I was unable to pursue it at all until nearly three and a half years later with epicodus!

For fun, I like to play music on the bass, drums, and guitar, sculpt, and play video games. I've played quite a few instruments over the years including piano and saxophone, but the three I've listed before are my main staples. My favorite form to sculpt is the hand, because I think its one ofthe most expressive parts of the body. Some of my all time favorite video games are anything belonging to the Dark Souls/Bloodborne franchise and more recently fighting games like Injustice 2, Soul Calibur VI, and Mortal Kombat X.
